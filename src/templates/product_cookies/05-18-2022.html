Hi team, With the official launch of Sibling Support on Tuesday, I wanted to kick off a new series, ***Product Cookies***, serving a digest of product updates, celebrations, learnings, and upcoming items on the horizon. Hopefully this will be useful, especially as we continue to grow as a team, to keep everyone connected and informed of Ello’s core product and the thought and processes that go into shaping our product’s direction. --- ## 💫 What’s new? ![Frame 6660.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/fe9921b4-ac1f-4bb7-b1df-f58efa26cd23/Frame_6660.png) ### **Official Siblings Support** 👨‍👩‍👦‍👦 That’s right, Ello now has sibling support. Horray! 🎉 Prior to this update, families with multiple children had to either have their children share a single Ello subscription, or create separate accounts for each children. Now, these families can have all their children under a single account/subscription, each with their own book selection and prize. Families can add additional profiles to share existing books for free, or pay an additional $13/mo to add additional book bundles. When a profile is added, users will be able to switch profiles through the in-app profile switcher demonstrated bellow. ![ezgif.com-gif-maker.gif](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/f43b1f02-1e42-4ee0-960d-6f90376e8a15/ezgif.com-gif-maker.gif)
